1.2 Types of Functions

Introduction

There are many different types of functions that will be covered in this course. In addition to solving equations, we will be examining their transformations, graphs, and applying them to real life scenarios.

Polynomial Functions

These functions should be the most familiar, which you've seen since grade 9. Polynomial functions include horizontal lines, linear graphs, parabolas, cubic graphs, and others. In an equation, polynomial functions have coefficients, constants, and the degrees of x must be positive integers only.
